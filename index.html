Our Story

Born from Roads
Less Travelled

FARBOUND was born from a simple obsession: the way a single scent can collapse the distance between who you are and where you've been. From the cedar-laced bazaars of old Marrakech to the salt winds of the Faroe Islands, every fragrance in our collection is a cartography of the senses.

We source our raw ingredients with ruthless intention — partnering with growers on six continents, hand-selecting resins, florals, and woods at their peak expression. The result is a perfume house that does not simply smell beautiful, but feels like somewhere.
