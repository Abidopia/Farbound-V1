Our Story

Made for Those
Who Wander

Some places stay with you long after you've left — in the warm resin of a market stall at dusk, the green bite of rain on mountain stone, the stillness of a forest you've only crossed once. FARBOUND bottles that feeling.

We travel far to find what cannot be manufactured. Petals hand-harvested at dawn. Roots pulled from volcanic earth. Woods that have grown slowly, quietly, for centuries. Then we distill these discoveries into fragrances that live on the skin like a memory of somewhere beautiful.
